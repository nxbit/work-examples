VOC Details
Metric Formula Comments
FCR FCR Success Call Count / FCR Evaluated Call Count

FCR Success Call Count {Divided By} FCR Evaluated Call Count		 FCR Success Calls are callers who did not call back with in the 168hr window. FCR Evaluated Calls are those which meet FCR Eligibility Requirements.
AHT (Total Talk Time + Total HOLD Time + Total ACW Time) / Total Calls Handled

Total Talk Time {plus} Total HOLD Time {plus} Total ACW Time {Divided By} Total Calls Handled		 The Month to Date value is calculated by adding up the totals for entire month and then preforming the formula.
VOC (Q1 Rate + Q2 Rate + Q3 Rate + Q4 Rate)/4

Q1 Rate {plus} Q2 Rate {plus} Q3 Rate {plus} Q4 Rate {Divided By} 4		 The Individual Passing Rates for each question are added up and divided by four in order to gain the Overall VOC. Essentially its the overall passing rate of any given question.
ASA Total Queue Time / Total Inbound Segments The Average Speed to Answer represents the amount of time an inbound call segment is in queue before it is answered by an Agent
Transfer Rate Total Segments Transferred / (Total Calls Handled + Total Outbound Segments)

Total Segments Transferred {Divided by} Total Calls Handled {plus} Total Outbound Calls		 Total Segments Transferred is made up of any call which does not end with the Agent and the segment leaves (transferred) the switch. This divided by the total number of segments handled (inbound and outbound) gives us our Transfer Rate
Containment Rate Total Segments marked FCR Eligible, Truck Roll: No, and FCR Success: Yes / Total FCR Eligible Calls

Total Segments FCR Eligible: Yes; Truck Roll: No; FCR Success: Yes {Divided By} Total FCR Eligible Calls		 Containment Rate answers the question out of all the FCR Eligible calls how many are we able to fix or contain.
